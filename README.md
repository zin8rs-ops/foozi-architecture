# Foozi

Foozi is a football intelligence platform that combines structured football data, prediction models and AI-generated insights.

The goal of the platform is to provide deeper understanding of football matches, teams and players through data-driven analysis.

Positioning

Football Intelligence Platform

Not a traditional livescore website.

---

# Platform Vision

Foozi transforms raw football data into meaningful insights.

Core capabilities include

match predictions  
team and player intelligence  
AI-generated insights  
data-driven match analysis  

The platform combines football statistics with AI systems to help users understand matches more deeply.

---

The Foozi platform consists of several layers.
