# Homepage Component Structure

The Foozi homepage is composed of several UI sections.

Each section is built from reusable design system components.

---

# Homepage Layout

Navigation
↓
Today’s Matches (Hero)
↓
Tactra Insights
↓
Community Picks
↓
AI Match Insights
↓
Trending Teams
↓
League Navigation
↓
Tomorrow Matches
↓
Footer

---

# Components Used

## MatchCard

Displays upcoming matches.

Content

teams  
logos  
kickoff time  
league  
tactra score  

---

## PredictionBar

Shows Tactra probabilities.

Example

Home Win 64%  
Draw 21%  
Away Win 15%

---

## InsightCard

Displays short AI insights.

Example

"Bayern scored in 9 consecutive home matches."

---

## CommunityPredictionCard

Shows prediction distribution.

Example

Home Win 54%  
Draw 21%  
Away Win 25%

---

## TeamCard

Displays trending teams.

Content

team logo  
team name  
link to team page

---

## LeagueNavigation

Links to major competitions.

Example

Bundesliga  
Premier League  
La Liga  
Serie A  

---

## MatchList

Displays tomorrow’s matches.

Content

team names  
match time  
league
