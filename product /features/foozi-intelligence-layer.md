# Foozi Intelligence Layer

The Foozi Intelligence Layer combines prediction models, statistical analysis and AI insights to generate intelligent football information.

The goal is to transform raw football data into actionable insights for fans and analysts.

---

# Core Intelligence Modules

The intelligence layer consists of several analytical systems.

Tactra Prediction Engine  
AI Insight Engine  
Advanced Statistics  
Team Strength Index  
Player Impact Rating  

These modules work together to generate football intelligence across the platform.

---

# 1 Tactra Prediction Engine

The prediction engine calculates match outcome probabilities.

Output

Home Win Probability  
Draw Probability  
Away Win Probability  

Example

Bayern vs Dortmund

Home Win — 64%  
Draw — 21%  
Away Win — 15%

The prediction model combines multiple statistical signals.

---

# 2 AI Insight Engine

The AI Insight Engine translates football statistics into natural language insights.

Examples

"Bayern scored in 9 consecutive home matches."

"Liverpool conceded only 2 goals in the last 6 matches."

These insights help users understand the context behind the numbers.

---

# 3 Advanced Statistics

The platform calculates advanced metrics that describe team and player performance.

Examples

expected goals (xG)  
shot conversion rate  
possession efficiency  
defensive stability  

These metrics are used by both the prediction engine and the insight engine.

---

# 4 Team Strength Index

The Team Strength Index estimates the relative strength of a team.

Signals include

recent form  
goal difference  
expected goals  
squad value  

Example

Bayern Munich  
Team Strength Index: 91

Borussia Dortmund  
Team Strength Index: 84

This score helps compare teams across competitions.

---

# 5 Player Impact Rating

The Player Impact Rating measures a player's influence on matches.

Signals include

goals  
assists  
expected goals contribution  
chances created  

Example

Erling Haaland  
Impact Rating: 9.2

Jamal Musiala  
Impact Rating: 8.7

---

The intelligence layer feeds insights across the entire platform.
