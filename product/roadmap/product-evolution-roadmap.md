# Foozi Product Evolution Roadmap

This roadmap describes how the Foozi platform evolves from a football data platform into a football intelligence platform.

---

# Phase 1 – Core Football Platform

Goal

Provide structured football data and match information.

Features

competition pages  
match pages  
team pages  
player pages  
standings  

---

# Phase 2 – Prediction Platform

Goal

Introduce match predictions and probability models.

Features

Tactra prediction engine  
match probability scores  
prediction visualizations  

---

# Phase 3 – Intelligence Platform

Goal

Provide deeper football insights powered by AI.

Features

AI insight engine  
team strength index  
player impact rating  
momentum scores  

---

# Phase 4 – Community Platform

Goal

Enable user participation and engagement.

Features

community predictions  
prediction leaderboards  
user profiles  

---

# Phase 5 – Advanced Intelligence

Goal

Provide advanced football analytics.

Features

match momentum analysis  
player comparisons  
advanced tactical insights
