# Foozi SEO & Growth Architecture

This document defines how the Foozi platform generates scalable SEO traffic.

The platform is designed to automatically generate a large number of structured football pages.

---

# SEO Page Universe

The platform generates several types of SEO pages.

Core page types

- competition pages
- match pages
- team pages
- player pages
- ranking pages

Each page type targets specific football search intent.

---

# Competition Pages

Example

/bundesliga
/premier-league
/champions-league

Content

- standings
- upcoming matches
- top players
- statistics
- insights

Competition pages act as hubs linking to teams, players and matches.

---

# Match Pages

Example

/match/bayern-vs-dortmund

Content

- match prediction
- lineups
- statistics
- AI insights
- timeline

Match pages are generated for every scheduled match.

Estimated scale

10 leagues × 380 matches

= 3800 pages per season

With historical data

20000+ match pages

---

# Team Pages

Example

/team/bayern-munich
/team/arsenal
/team/real-madrid

Content

- squad
- recent matches
- team statistics
- AI insights

These pages act as important SEO entry points.

---

# Player Pages

Example

/player/jamal-musiala
/player/erling-haaland

Content

- player statistics
- match history
- performance trends
- insights

Player pages attract search queries for individual footballers.

---

# Ranking Pages

Ranking pages are high-value SEO pages.

Examples

/top-scorers
/most-assists
/market-values
/clean-sheets

Content

leaderboards  
player rows  
statistics  

Ranking pages attract evergreen football search traffic.

---

# Internal Linking Strategy

The platform uses strong internal linking between entities.

Example

Competition Page
↓
Team Pages
↓
Player Pages
↓
Match Pages

This improves crawlability and SEO performance.

---

# AI Content Layer

The AI Insight Engine generates contextual content.

Examples

match previews  
team insights  
player performance summaries  

This allows scalable content generation without manual writing.
